about

normuseum

normuseum is a first-person 3D voxel gallery for the Normies NFT collection on ethereum mainnet. paste any wallet address or ENS name and walk through your collection on the walls of a minimal white-cube gallery.

each normie is rendered as a high-quality voxel relief, with dark pixels physically extruded from the canvas to create a tactile, sculptural effect. a single room grows longer to host collections of any size, with marble finishes, moulding, and skylight detail throughout.
