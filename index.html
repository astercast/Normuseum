normuseum

normuseum is a first-person 3D voxel gallery for the Normies NFT collection on ethereum mainnet. paste any wallet address or ENS name — multiple wallets supported, comma-separated — and walk through your collection on the walls of a minimal white-cube gallery. each normie is rendered as a high-quality voxel relief, with dark pixels physically extruded from the canvas to create a tactile, sculptural effect.
